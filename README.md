## About Plyse Professional Services

Plyse Professional Services is a performance consulting firm that works with leaders and organizations to strengthen the connection between **performance behaviours and business results**.

For nearly two decades, we have supported executives and leadership teams across Africa and beyond to build high-performance organizations by addressing the systemic and behavioural factors that drive or constrain performance.

Our work focuses on helping organizations move from **performance ambiguity to performance clarity**.

---

## What We Do

We help organizations diagnose performance gaps, strengthen leadership accountability, and align teams around the priorities that truly move the business forward.

Our services include:

- **Performance Consulting** – diagnosing organizational performance gaps and designing corrective systems  
- **Leadership Coaching** – strengthening leadership effectiveness and accountability  
- **Training Programmes** – building the skills and mindsets required for high performance  
- **Executive Facilitation** – guiding leadership teams through strategic conversations and decisions

## Our Focus

We help organizations address common performance challenges such as:

- Strategic drift
- Lack of ownership and accountability
- Misaligned effort across teams
- Over-reliance on lagging indicators
- Avoidance of critical performance conversations

## Resource Overview

This repository hosts a digital resource developed by Plyse Professional Services to support leaders and organizations in strengthening the connection between performance behaviours and business results.

The resource is designed as a practical tool that can be used during leadership discussions, performance reviews, strategy conversations, or organizational diagnostics.

It is intended to help organizations move beyond surface-level performance conversations and toward clearer identification of the behaviours, systems, and decisions that influence business outcomes.

The resource is hosted online to provide easy and secure access for clients, training participants, and leadership teams.

## Who This Resource Is For

This resource is designed primarily for:

• CEOs and senior executives responsible for organizational performance  
• Leadership teams conducting strategy or performance reviews  
• Managers seeking clearer accountability and alignment within teams  

## How to Use This Tool

To use this resource:

1. Open the resource using the secure link provided below.
2. Review the questions, prompts, or diagnostic elements included in the tool.
3. Use the resource individually or during a leadership discussion to surface performance insights.
4. Identify areas where performance behaviours, priorities, or ownership may need adjustment.
5. Translate insights from the resource into clear actions or follow-up conversations.

For best results, the resource should be used as part of a structured leadership discussion focused on improving organizational performance and aligning effort with strategic priorities.

## When to Use This Resource

This resource is most valuable when leadership teams need clearer insight into organizational performance and the behaviours that influence business results.

It can be used in situations such as:

**Quarterly Performance Reviews**  
To move beyond reporting numbers and focus on the underlying drivers of performance.

**Strategic Alignment Conversations**  
When leaders need to confirm that teams are executing against the right priorities.

**Leadership Team Discussions**  
To surface assumptions, clarify ownership, and strengthen accountability for results.

**Performance Diagnostics**  
When there is a sense that performance is off track but the specific causes are not yet clear.

**Organizational Reset Moments**  
At the start of a new quarter, strategy cycle, or transformation initiative.

When used well, the resource helps leadership teams shift from general performance discussions to more precise conversations about the behaviours, decisions, and systems that determine results.
