Linking performance behaviours to business results

Resource Overview

This resource has been developed by Plyse Professional Services to support leaders and organizations in strengthening the connection between performance behaviours and business results.

It provides a structured way for leadership teams to surface performance gaps, clarify ownership, and focus effort on the activities that truly move the organization forward.

Who This Resource Is For

  • CEOs and senior executives responsible for organizational performance
  • Leadership teams conducting strategy or performance reviews
  • Managers seeking clearer accountability and alignment
  • Organizations participating in Plyse consulting, coaching, or training programmes

How to Use This Tool

  1. Open the resource using the link below.
  2. Review the diagnostic questions and prompts.
  3. Use the tool during leadership discussions or strategy reviews.
  4. Identify performance gaps, ownership issues, or misaligned priorities.
  5. Translate insights into clear action plans.

When to Use This Resource

  • Quarterly performance reviews
  • Leadership strategy sessions
  • Performance diagnostics
  • Organizational reset moments
  • Leadership alignment discussions
